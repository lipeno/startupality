Risks by exposure:

Green risks are not very big. Acknowledge green risks, and continue to monitor them, but dedicate fewer resources to mitigating them than the yellow risks.

Yellow risks are in between green and red risks and depending on the case they can be very important or not.

Red risks are important and the plan to deal with them should be done immediately.
